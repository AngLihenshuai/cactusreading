About The Production

Through the increase in the popularity of online reading, many people rely on online dictionaries to further understand the text. However, looking up a word manually is a great inconvenience as well as a form of distraction. This program develops a chrome extension to automatically detect the difficult words for each user, and provide the words’ associated definition with a mouse hover.

The chrome extension can be customized by adding and removing personal difficult words and personal easy words. Also, the chrome extension offers a deeper level of analytic, including the system analyzing part of speech of the world, to further understand the definition of a selected word or sentence. The chrome extension is applied to a school/work setting in order to improve the working efficiency by providing a simple model to analyze the word definition; it is also useful for casual reading, especially to those that aren’t fluent in English.

Services

Best Service

Cactus reading provides you the Best service to boost your reading experience! The way you can use the feature is explained below. Reading with cact-us!

Scan and Define
The main feature of the app! Through an intuitive user interface on the Chrome extension pop up, the program will highlight all of the difficult words on the webpage and provide the definition of it adjacent to the word. The highlight is in blue and the text is in white, which provides a clear color contrast for the reading experience.
Customize dataset
The program stores user's information on a database, where there is a default wordbank to determine the uncommon word, and the users can customize their own common/uncommon words, which makes the app more accessible. This feature can be accessed through highlighting a word and use a right click dropdown menu. The future development for the dataset may be using natural language processing to determine a more accurate uncommon word to each user without manual adding/removing.
Deep Analysis
Have you ever looked up a word and don't know which definition to use? This feature covers the problem most of the time! Selecting a sentence or a paragraph and visit the right click dropdown menu to access the deep analysis feature, and it will you the part of the speech of the word in a given context.
